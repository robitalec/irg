# v 0.1.0 (2019-01-04)
## Initial release

One vignette ('Getting started with irg') and: 

Five filtering functions:
* `filter_qa`
* `filter_winter`
* `filter_roll`
* `filter_top`

Two scaling functions:
* `scale_doy`
* `scale_ndvi`

Three modeling functions:
* `model_start`
* `model_params`
* `model_ndvi`

One instantaneous rate of green-up function:
* `calc_irg`

Two meta functions:
* `filter_ndvi`
* `irg`
